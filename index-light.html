DN Technoverse 2023 aims to provide a platform for 2nd & 3rd year students (2025 & 2026 graduates) from engineering colleges to collaborate, innovate and showcase their problem-solving skills. We firmly believe that diversity and inclusion is essential for generating unique perspectives and solutions.
